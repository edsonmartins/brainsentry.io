## Domain Strategy

### Primary Domain Options

**Preferred:**
- `.ai` - Best for AI products (premium but worth it)
- `.io` - Tech standard (good availability)
- `.com` - Traditional, trusted

**Acceptable:**
- `.dev` - Developer-focused
- `.tech` - Technology emphasis
- `.app` - SaaS products

**Avoid:**
- `.net`, `.org` - Less relevant
- `.br` - Limits international appeal
- `.xyz`, `.co` - Less professional

### Pricing Expectations

```
.com    $10-15/year (standard)
        $1,000-50,000 (premium if taken)

.ai     $50-100/year (standard)
        Higher for premium

.io     $30-50/year (standard)

.dev    $12-15/year
```
